Adjective suppletion

  • Restrictions on Adjective suppletion.
    • AAA: smart ~ smarter ~ smartest
    • ABB: good ~ better ~ best
    • ABC: bonus ~ melior ~ optimus
    • *AAB: good ~ gooder ~ best
    • *ABA: good ~ better ~ goodest
  • Derived the missing patterns through containment and VI.

Case Syncretisms

  • Zompí's (2019) generalisation:

A non-accidental syncretism cannot cover both the nominative/absolutive and an oblique case (other than the genitive) without also covering another core structural case (accusative or ergative).

  • That is: in syncretism *ABA holds.

Tripartite

  • Zompí claims that it is not the case that anything goes.
  • There is no syncretism targeting nominative and another oblique.
  • The containment is then as follows:

unmarked ⊂ accusative/ergative ⊂ oblique

  • There is no ordering between accusative and ergative.
  • In other words, they are both treated as part of the same meta-class, dependent case.
